Our Story

Amber Rice
CEO & Co-Founder
Steve Ward
CRO & Co-Founder

Built by people who've been in the weeds.

Ember Bots was born from a simple observation: the gap between what technology can do and what most small businesses actually have access to is enormous — and it's costing them.

Amber Rice and Steve Ward founded Ember Bots with a shared conviction — that the AI tools transforming enterprise companies shouldn't be out of reach for the small businesses that need them most. Between them, they bring deep experience in business operations, revenue growth, and customer relationships to every engagement.

They've seen firsthand how much time and energy gets consumed by tasks that don't require human judgment — the follow-up emails, the data entry, the scheduling, the report generation. Tasks that eat hours every week and quietly compound into competitive disadvantage.

The tools to fix this exist. Large companies have teams of engineers deploying them. Small businesses don't. That's the gap Ember Bots was built to close.

We bring enterprise-grade AI automation to small businesses — without the enterprise price tag or the enterprise complexity. We handle the technical heavy lifting so you can focus on growing the business you built.

We're based in Dallas, TX and work with businesses across industries. If you're ready to stop doing the work that shouldn't require you, let's talk.
