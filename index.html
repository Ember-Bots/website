The Problem

Where small businesses lose time and momentum.

Most small businesses are not losing because they lack hustle. They're losing time to slow follow-up, manual admin, scattered systems, and work that should not require owner attention.

Ember Bots is built to remove that drag, with automation systems that handle routine operational work faster and more consistently.

  • Leads sit too long before someone responds
  • Missed calls turn into missed revenue
  • Scheduling and follow-up happen manually
  • Teams re-enter the same information in multiple places
  • Onboarding, routing, and admin work steal time from service and growth

Automation Systems

Automation systems we deploy.

Systems designed to help small teams respond faster, stay organized, and reduce operational drag. Each one is configured to fit your business, your team, and your workflow.

Capture

Lead Response Automation

Capture inbound leads from forms, calls, chat, and email, respond quickly, route them internally, and make sure no opportunity sits untouched.

Customer Message Routing

Unify customer messages from email, SMS, website chat, and social channels, classify intent, and route each conversation to the right person.

Coordinate

Scheduling & Reminder Automation

Book appointments, send confirmations, manage reminders, and handle reschedules automatically so your team is not chasing the calendar by hand.

Client Onboarding Automation

Collect intake information, request missing documents, send welcome steps, and trigger the right internal tasks so onboarding feels organized from day one.

Internal Task Orchestration

Turn emails, forms, and operational events into assigned tasks, reminders, and daily summaries so your team knows what needs attention next.

Operate

Follow-Up Automation

Keep prospects, customers, and internal workflows moving with structured reminders, nudges, and escalation rules that run on schedule.

Back Office Admin Automation

Reduce repetitive admin work around intake, document collection, task routing, status tracking, and recurring operational work that drains your team.

Executive Daily Briefing

Pull together email, calendar, tasks, leads, and team updates into a clean daily briefing so owners and operators start the day with clarity.

Each system is deployed from a proven automation template and configured to your business, your team, your tools, and your escalation rules. Additional systems for document collection, recurring admin routines, inbox triage, and internal reporting are also available.
