Ember Bots Master Services Agreement

Draft for attorney review. Incorporated into each signed Order Form.

1. Agreement

This Master Services Agreement governs services provided by Ember Bots, [legal entity type and state] to the customer identified in an order form. Each signed order form, statement of work, quote accepted by signature, or similar written ordering document is an Order Form.

This Agreement and the applicable Order Form together form the complete agreement for the services ordered. If an Order Form conflicts with this Agreement, the Order Form controls for that specific order.

2. Services

Ember Bots provides custom AI chatbot setup, AI agent setup, automation services, workflow integrations, monitoring, and related support services as described in the applicable Order Form. Ember Bots may determine technical methods, tools, architecture, models, vendors, hosting approach, workflows, and implementation details unless the Order Form states otherwise.

3. Activation

Activation means the first customer-facing or operational automation, chatbot, or AI agent under the applicable Order Form is live and available for Customer's use. Activation may occur when Ember Bots determines that the delivered service materially meets the agreed scope, even if Customer later requests changes, refinements, or enhancements.

4. Fees and Payment

  • Setup fees are due upon signing.
  • The first monthly subscription payment is due at Activation.
  • Monthly fees recur monthly after Activation.
  • Third-party tools, software, model usage, telephony, messaging, hosting, API, advertising, and similar charges may be billed through to Customer if listed in the Order Form or reasonably required for the services.
  • Fees already paid are non-refundable, except for SLA credits expressly provided in this Agreement or as required by law.

Ember Bots may suspend services if any invoice is more than seven days overdue. Customer remains responsible for unpaid fees during suspension.

5. Initial Term, Renewal, and Cancellation

Monthly services have a three-month minimum initial term beginning at Activation unless the Order Form states a different term. After the initial term, monthly services renew month-to-month unless either party gives at least 30 days' written notice of cancellation.

Cancellation does not relieve Customer of payment obligations for fees already incurred, the remaining minimum initial term, pass-through third-party costs, or approved additional work.

6. Customer Responsibilities

Customer will provide timely access to systems, accounts, tools, data, contacts, business rules, credentials, content, approvals, and other materials reasonably needed for Ember Bots to perform the services.

  • Customer is responsible for the accuracy and legality of Customer-provided content, data, prompts, offers, claims, instructions, workflows, business rules, and policies.
  • Customer is responsible for reviewing outputs and workflows before relying on them in regulated, high-risk, or customer-facing contexts.
  • Customer must maintain any licenses, consents, notices, permissions, and legal authority required for Customer's business, customer communications, data use, and third-party tools.

7. Change Requests

Minor changes consistent with the agreed scope may be included at Ember Bots' discretion. Material changes, new workflows, new integrations, revised business logic, expanded use cases, additional support above the included support amount, and work outside the agreed scope may require written approval and may affect price, timeline, or subscription fees.

8. Support

The monthly subscription includes the fixed support amount stated in the Order Form. Support may include monitoring, troubleshooting, reasonable adjustments, operational questions, and maintenance of covered live automations or agents. Support above the included amount may be billed at the rate stated in the Order Form or at Ember Bots' then-current support rate.

9. Service Level Credits

Customer may request a partial monthly service credit if a covered live agent or automation is materially unavailable due to an Ember Bots-controlled system failure.

SLA credits do not apply to planned maintenance, third-party outages, Customer-caused issues, development or non-production systems, force majeure events, circumstances outside Ember Bots' reasonable control, or periods when Customer is overdue on payment.

10. Ownership

Customer owns Customer's business data, customer data, content, brand assets, and materials Customer provides. Subject to full payment, Customer receives the right to use configured live deliverables for Customer's internal business operations.

Ember Bots retains ownership of its pre-existing materials, platform components, templates, prompts, workflows, agent frameworks, automation libraries, scripts, know-how, methodologies, reusable components, internal tools, configuration patterns, and independently developed improvements.

11. Third-Party Tools

The services may depend on third-party tools, software, APIs, models, hosting providers, messaging services, telephony providers, CRM systems, calendar systems, email services, payment processors, and other vendors. Ember Bots may bill third-party costs through to Customer unless the Order Form states otherwise.

12. AI and Automation Limitations

AI agents, chatbots, language models, and automations may produce inaccurate, incomplete, unexpected, delayed, or unsuitable outputs. Customer is responsible for supervising use of the services and deciding when human review is required.

The services are not a substitute for legal, medical, financial, insurance, tax, compliance, or other regulated professional advice. Ember Bots does not guarantee revenue, profits, sales, leads, appointments, conversion rates, search rankings, advertising results, operational savings, or any other business outcome.

13. Confidentiality

Each party may receive non-public business, technical, financial, customer, operational, or strategic information from the other. Each party will use the other's Confidential Information only to perform or receive the services, will protect it using reasonable care, and will not disclose it except to those who need to know for the services and are bound by confidentiality obligations or professional duties.

14. Data

Customer authorizes Ember Bots to access, process, transmit, and store Customer data as reasonably needed to provide, secure, maintain, improve, troubleshoot, and support the services. Customer is responsible for required consents, permissions, notices, and legal rights.

15. Compliance and Prohibited Uses

Customer will not use the services to violate law, infringe rights, mislead people, send unlawful communications, transmit harmful code, bypass platform rules, make prohibited regulated decisions, or engage in deceptive, abusive, or unauthorized activity.

16. Disclaimers

Except as expressly stated in this Agreement or an Order Form, the services are provided "as is" and "as available." Ember Bots disclaims implied warranties to the fullest extent permitted by law and does not warrant uninterrupted, error-free, secure, or universally compatible services.

17. Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, Ember Bots will not be liable for indirect, incidental, special, consequential, exemplary, punitive, or lost-profit damages, or for loss of revenue, goodwill, data, business opportunity, or business interruption.

Ember Bots' total aggregate liability will not exceed the fees paid by Customer for the affected services during the three months before the event giving rise to the claim.

18. Indemnity

Customer will defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Ember Bots from claims, damages, liabilities, losses, costs, and expenses arising from Customer's content, data, instructions, misuse of the services, violation of law, violation of third-party rights, or breach of this Agreement.

19. Suspension and Termination

Ember Bots may suspend or terminate services if Customer fails to pay, materially breaches this Agreement, creates legal or security risk, misuses the services, violates third-party platform rules, or asks Ember Bots to perform unlawful or prohibited work.

20. Electronic Signatures

The parties agree to conduct transactions electronically. Electronic signatures, PDF signatures, click-to-sign workflows, and electronic records are intended to have the same legal effect as handwritten signatures and paper records to the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, including the Texas Uniform Electronic Transactions Act where applicable.

21. Governing Law and Venue

This Agreement and all Order Forms are governed by Texas law, without regard to conflict of law rules. The parties consent to exclusive jurisdiction and venue in the state or federal courts located in Texas unless they agree in writing to another dispute resolution process.

22. Notices

23. General Terms

Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other party's written consent, except to an affiliate or successor in connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of substantially all assets. Neither party is liable for delay or failure caused by events outside its reasonable control. If any provision is unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in effect.

24. Signatures

This Agreement may be accepted by signing an Order Form that incorporates it, signing this Agreement directly, or another written acceptance method agreed by the parties.
