Core Message

What Ember Bots does

  • helps service businesses stop losing good leads
  • improves response speed and follow-up consistency
  • creates a clearer operating system for revenue capture
  • starts with a Missed Revenue Snapshot
Why this matters

The real problem is not always lead generation. It is what happens after the lead arrives.

Small service businesses often lose revenue through missed calls, slow web-lead response, inconsistent estimate follow-up, and low visibility into what is actually being worked. Ember Bots is positioned as the system that tightens that gap.

Pain 1
Missed calls that never turn into conversations
Pain 2
Forms that sit too long without response
Pain 3
Estimates that go cold before booking
Pain 4
Office chaos with weak visibility and inconsistent follow-up
