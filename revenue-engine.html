FAQ

Common questions before the demo.

Is this software or a service?

Both. Ember Bots offers a software-backed system, and for VIP clients we also provide hands-on setup, management, and optimization.

Does this replace my sales team?

No. It gives your team a stronger system for prospecting, outreach, follow-up, and booking. It works best with clear human ownership on qualified opportunities.

What channels does it support?

Email first, with LinkedIn, scheduling, CRM routing, and additional follow-up channels depending on the plan and use case.

Can Ember manage the campaigns for us?

Yes. That is the VIP offer and the recommended primary sales motion.

Who is this best for?

Owner-led service businesses and B2B teams that need more qualified conversations without building a full outbound operations function internally.

How fast can this launch?

Typically within days for self-serve onboarding and within 1–3 weeks for managed deployments, depending on readiness, domains, offer clarity, and workflow complexity.
